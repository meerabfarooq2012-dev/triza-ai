2️⃣ XOR Binding Do concepts jodna

XOR = CPU ka sabse tez operation. Do vectors ko XOR karte hain to ek naya vector banta hai jo dono ka "combination" hai. Yeh HDC ka dil hai.

Result: A XOR B
XOR result pehla hissa: Pehle Vector A aur B banao
💡 XOR ka jadoo: A XOR B XOR A = B (wapas mil jata hai!). Isiliye HDC memory "holographic" hai — har cheez recover ho sakti hai.

3️⃣ Hamming Distance Kitne bits alag?

Do vectors mein kitne bits alag hain — yeh "Hamming Distance" kehte hain. Kam distance = zyada similar. HDC isi se pehchanta hai.

Result: Pehle dono vectors banao
💡 256 bits mein do RANDOM vectors ka distance ≈ 128 (50%) hota hai. Agar distance < 100 hai to "similar" maana jata hai.

4️⃣ Memory & Pehchanna Asli AI!

Ab asli kaam. Memory mein kuch words store karo. Phir naya word do — AI batayega kaunsa memory wala word sabse zyada match karta hai.

💡 Har word ka apna random 256-bit "naam" ban jata hai. Ab neeche test karo.

5️⃣ Test — AI Pehchane Ga? Recall

Memory mein jo words hain, un mein se ek likho. AI ko nahi pata kaunsa likhogi — woh distance check karke pehchanega. Yahi HDC ka pehchanna hai!
