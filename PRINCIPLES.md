## 🌑 The O-H-C-E Framework (Short Form)

```
Observe → Hierarchy → Causality → Emotion
```

**Core rules:**
- **Emotion** observation ka input NAHI — observation ka **output** hai.
- **Curiosity** instance pe depend nahi karta — **category** pe depend karta hai.
- AI sirf data nigalne se NAHI seekhta. AI ko cheez **dobara bana kar dikha** — tab seekha.
- **Knowledge** = kese jora (verb), kya dikhta (noun) nahi.
- **Connection** tab valid hai jab uska output khud ek raw material ban jaye — jo agle connection mein input ban sakta hai.

## 🪨 Axioms (Foundational Truths)

### Axiom 0: Observation is the root of all cognition.
> Observation = ek **stream** hai, single moment nahi.

### Axiom 1: Agency = Resistance
> Jo cheez pakarne par **resist** karti hai — wo khud move karti hai.
> Jo resist nahi karti — wo move hoti hai kisi aur ki wajah se.
>
> `Agency(O) = self_caused / (self_caused + other_caused)`
